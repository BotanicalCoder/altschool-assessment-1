Fluffy Banana Pancakes

A tasty breakfast recipe with ripe bananas, soft pancakes, and a hint of vanilla.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 4 pancakes

Difficulty: Easy

Recipe Image

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mash the bananas until smooth with small lumps remaining.
  2. Stir in the egg, milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. Pour the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and stir gently.
  5. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little oil or butter.
  6. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form, then flip the pancake.
  7. Cook the other side until golden brown and transfer to a plate.

Notes

For extra flavor, add a pinch of cinnamon or top with fresh berries and maple syrup.

Tip: Use very ripe bananas for the sweetest, fluffiest pancakes.

Nutrition Facts

Approximate values per serving: 250 calories, 8g protein, 35g carbohydrates, 8g fat.
